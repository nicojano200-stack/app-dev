# app-dev# 🍉 My Favorite K-Drama: Twinkling Watermelon

## Overview
**Twinkling Watermelon**  is a South Korean drama
that aired on *tvN* in **2023**. It is a heartwarming story that blends
*romance*, *music*, and *time travel* into one unforgettable series.

---

## Synopsis

> "What if you could go back in time and meet your parents when they were young?"

Ha Eun-gyeol, a high school student born into a deaf family, suddenly travels
back to **1995** where he meets his father as a teenager. Along the way, he
discovers love, music, and the truth about his family's past.

---

## Why I Love It

-  Amazing live music performances throughout the series
-  Emotional and touching family story
-  Perfect balance of comedy and drama
-  Outstanding acting from the entire cast
-  Unique and creative plot that keeps you hooked
